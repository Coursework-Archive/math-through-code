Question 61

$$ x + (y - 1)^2 = 0 $$

$$ (y - 1)^2 = -x $$

$$ y - 1 = \pm \sqrt{-x} $$

$$ y = 1 \pm \sqrt{-x} $$

Since the problem asks for the bottom half of the curve, we choose the negative branch:

$$ \boxed{y = 1 - \sqrt{-x}} $$

For the square root to be defined:

$$ -x \ge 0 \;\Rightarrow\; x \le 0 $$

$$ \boxed{\text{Domain} = (-\infty,\,0]} $$

Question 62

$$ x^2 + (y - 2)^2 = 4 $$

$$ (y - 2)^2 = 4 - x^2 $$

$$ y - 2 = \pm \sqrt{4 -x^2 } $$

$$ y = 2 \pm \sqrt{4 - x^2 } $$

Since the problem asks for the top half of the curve, we choose the positive branch:

$$ \boxed{y = 2 + \sqrt{4 - x^2 }} $$

For the square root to be defined:

$$ 4 - x^2 \ge 0 \;\Rightarrow\; -2 \le x \le 2 $$

$$ \boxed{\text{Domain} = [-2,\,2]} $$
